Closing out 2024, we count the year's generative AI scoreboard among our clients: many pilots, a handful in production, and a clear pattern separating the two groups.

The pilots that stalled treated the model as the product. The ones that shipped treated it as one component in a system, surrounded by retrieval over governed data, evaluation suites that run on every change, cost and latency budgets, human review on consequential outputs, and honest fallbacks when confidence is low.

That surrounding system is ordinary engineering, and it is most of the work. Our expectation for 2025: less novelty, more accountability. Teams will be asked what these systems cost, how they fail, and who is watching. Good. That is what production means.
