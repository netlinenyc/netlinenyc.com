A brief company note. As of this month, our engineering team collectively holds more than forty professional-level certifications across AWS, Google Cloud, and Microsoft Azure, including architect, security, and data engineering specializations, along with several Kubernetes and Terraform credentials.

We are deliberate about this investment. Certifications do not make a consultant, and we evaluate hires on delivery record first. But maintaining current credentials across all three major platforms keeps our recommendations grounded in what each platform offers today rather than what it offered when we last looked. Every NetLine consultant gets study time on the clock and exam costs covered.

Congratulations to the eleven team members who earned new credentials this year.
