Halfway through 2026, a pattern is clear in our inbound work: the questions have shifted from adoption to governance. Few clients are asking whether to use cloud, Kubernetes, or AI tooling. Nearly all are asking how to control what they already have.

The three engagement types dominating our calendar: cost and capacity governance for AI workloads that grew faster than their budgets, consolidation of platform sprawl accumulated during the 2023-2024 build-everything years, and audit readiness as security questionnaires from enterprise customers grow sharper.

We read this as a healthy market correction. The experimental phase produced real capability and real mess, in roughly equal measure. The next few years of our work will be about keeping the former and retiring the latter.
