We recently completed an eight-week cost optimization for a digital media company whose AWS bill had doubled in eighteen months while traffic grew far more modestly.

No heroics were required. We right-sized a fleet of instances provisioned for a launch-day peak that never recurred, moved cold media assets to archival storage tiers, replaced an always-on staging environment with one that sleeps outside business hours, fixed a logging misconfiguration that was writing debug output at production volume, and restructured commitment coverage around the workloads that survived the cleanup.

Net result: 34 percent reduction in monthly spend, zero architecture changes, zero user-facing impact. The deeper fix was a weekly cost review the client now runs without us.
