NetLine NYC is a technology consulting firm based in New York City. We work in three areas — application design, cloud migration, and cloud platform consulting — and deliberately nothing else.

We started the firm because we kept watching the same pattern: companies hiring large consultancies for cloud work, getting a rotating cast of junior staff, and ending up with architectures nobody on either side fully understood. Our answer was to stay small, staff every engagement with senior people, and limit the number of projects we take on at once.

## Who we work with

Our clients range from venture-backed startups to Fortune 500 enterprises, with a concentration in mid-size businesses — companies large enough to have real infrastructure and real stakes, but without an in-house platform team for every problem. We've delivered work across fintech, healthcare, legal, logistics, retail, manufacturing, media, insurance, and the public sector.

## How we work

- **Senior-only staffing.** The people who scope the engagement are the people who do the work.
- **Platform-neutral.** We hold deep expertise across Google Cloud, AWS, and Azure, and we recommend the platform that fits the workload — not the one that fits a reseller agreement.
- **Fixed scope, honest estimates.** We'd rather decline a project than win it with an estimate we don't believe.
- **We stay through stabilization.** Migrations and launches end when the system is boring, not when it goes live.

## Where we are

We're headquartered in New York City and work with clients across North America and Europe. Most engagements are remote-first with on-site time where it counts: discovery, cutover, and launch.
